# Demo Briefs — Tuesday's Stage Performance

Hand-crafted briefs for the live demo. Each is **pre-tested**, has a **clear narrative**,
and will produce a confident response from the fine-tuned Llama 3.2 3B model.

## Selection criteria

- Must hit a **culturally-specific** template (so "we trained on 74 countries" lands)
- Must use **persona-aware** language (so "architectural reasoning" lands)
- Must produce a response that **mentions real local features** (room names, ceiling
  height, layout flow) — not generic corporate speak
- Must have **at least 1 strong fall-back** in case the top pick disappoints

## The 7 demo briefs

### 1. The "global library" hook (opening brief)
> *"Couple in their 30s, looking for a 1-bedroom apartment with historic character, Mediterranean feel, around 50-55 m². We work from home a few days a week."*

**Expected top picks:** Athens Monastiraki, Florence Oltrarno, Lisbon Bairro Alto, Sevilla Triana

**Why it's good for demo:** Shows multi-country results, native room names (Salón / Soggiorno / Saloni / Σαλόνι), historic-era reasoning.

---

### 2. The "local convention" demo
> *"Looking for a 2 BHK in South India for a young family. Good cross-ventilation, near schools. Mid-range budget, around 75 m²."*

**Expected top picks:** Trichy Ponnagar, Madurai Bypass Road, Coimbatore RS Puram, Mysore Vijayanagar

**Why it's good for demo:** Shows India-specific terminology (BHK, "vastu", Tamil Nadu), demonstrates persona-aware reasoning ("master + bedroom 2 layout gives kids and parents acoustic separation").

---

### 3. The "lifestyle constraint" demo
> *"I'm a remote worker, video calls daily. Need a quiet place to work from. 2-bed apartment in Berlin, prefer Altbau character with high ceilings."*

**Expected top picks:** Berlin Prenzlauer Eisenbahnwohnung, Berlin Wilhelminian Altbau, Berlin Friedrichshain Altbau, München Schwabing 2-Zimmer

**Why it's good for demo:** Shows persona-aware reasoning ("the second bedroom can become a real home office"), German-language room names (Diele, Wohnzimmer, Schlafzimmer, Küche), and architectural era awareness (Altbau, Wilhelminian).

---

### 4. The "small space efficiency" demo
> *"Single executive, only in the city ~10 days a month. Want a polished pied-à-terre — small studio in Düsseldorf, easy lock-and-leave."*

**Expected top picks:** Düsseldorf Pempelfort Single, Berlin Compact Studio, Hamburg StPauli Loft-Studio

**Why it's good for demo:** Niche persona that's clearly addressed, model recognizes "lock-and-leave" architectural framing.

---

### 5. The "edge case" demo (size-vs-style trade-off)
> *"Family of four, looking for a 3-bed in the UK with Victorian character. Around 100 m², ground floor preferred for the kids."*

**Expected top picks:** London Edwardian Terrace, Coventry post-war semi, Newcastle 3-bed, Edinburgh New Town

**Why it's good for demo:** UK-specific (terraced houses, Edwardian/Victorian eras), family persona, demonstrates how the model balances multiple constraints.

---

### 6. The "geographically flexible" demo
> *"Young couple, first apartment together. We cook a lot and entertain occasionally. Prefer separate kitchen, 1-bed, somewhere in Southern Europe."*

**Expected top picks:** Florence Oltrarno, Naples Chiaia, Sevilla Triana, Lisbon Bairro Alto

**Why it's good for demo:** Cross-country comparison with honest trade-offs, persona-aware ("entertain"), multi-language room name parade.

---

### 7. The "show-off" demo (everything at once)
> *"I'm an academic researcher with too many books. 2-bedroom apartment, quiet, lots of wall space, somewhere in Central Europe. Around 80-100 m², high ceilings preferred."*

**Expected top picks:** Warsaw Mokotów Kamienica, Budapest Neoklassicista, Prague Vinohrady, Vienna Altbau

**Why it's good for demo:** Hits Eastern European templates (Polish, Czech, Hungarian room names — Salon, Sypialnia, etc.), academic persona reasoning, multilingual elegance.

## Pre-demo checklist

- [ ] Top pick is from the right country
- [ ] Top 4 includes ≥3 culturally-relevant alternatives
- [ ] Reasoning mentions specific room names, ceiling height, or wall thickness
- [ ] Response time <8 seconds
- [ ] No hallucinated template IDs
